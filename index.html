About Our School

Kids Growing Play School (KGPS) is a premier educational institution located in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh. Established with the vision of providing quality early childhood education, we cater to children from Play Group to 3rd Standard.

Our mission is to create a safe, stimulating and nurturing environment where children can develop their cognitive, social, emotional and physical skills through play-based learning.

At KGPS, we believe that every child is unique and deserves individual attention to help them reach their full potential.

Child-Centered

Our curriculum is designed around the needs and interests of each child.

Qualified Staff

Our teachers are trained in early childhood education and development.

Safe Environment

Secure campus with CCTV surveillance and strict safety protocols.

Holistic Development

We focus on cognitive, physical, social and emotional growth.

Our Academic Programs

Play Group (1.5-2.5 yrs)

Our play group program focuses on sensory activities, basic motor skills, and social interaction through play.

Nursery (2.5-3.5 yrs)

Introduction to basic concepts through fun activities, storytelling, rhymes and creative play.

Primary (1st-3rd)

Structured learning with emphasis on literacy, numeracy, environmental studies and creative expression.

Admission Process

Join Our KGPS Family

Admissions are open for the academic session 2023-24 for all classes from Play Group to 3rd Standard.

Process:

  1. Fill out the registration form (online or offline)
  2. School visit and interaction with the principal
  3. Submission of required documents
  4. Payment of fees

Documents Required:

  • Birth Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card (if available)
  • 2 Passport Size Photos
  • Previous School Report Card (for 1st-3rd)
